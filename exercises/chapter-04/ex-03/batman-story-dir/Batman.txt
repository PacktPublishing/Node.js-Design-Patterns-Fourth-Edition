* Real name: Bruce Wayne
* Identity: A rich Gotham City billionaire who witnessed his parents’ murder as a child and vowed to fight crime.
* No superpowers: He’s human—his “powers” are intense training, genius-level intellect, detective skills, and cutting-edge tech.
* Symbol: The bat represents fear—he uses it to terrify criminals.
* Mission: Protect Gotham City from villains like the Joker, Penguin, Two-Face, etc.
